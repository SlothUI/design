About Me

Hey! I'm Sloth, a 21 year old Canadian designer and developer of 4 years with 3 years dedicated to User Interface and User Experience design. I've been into games and creation all my life, now using Roblox to funnel my creativity and passion in order to share what I care about with others, while making it high quality and worthwhile for all players. I give a rather unique, more artistic approach and innovative functionality in my designs compared to almost all others on the platform, leading to an immersive and special identity for the games I work on. When I work on a project, I focus on having a thorough consistency with the game's atmosphere and purpose of each component, bringing the player into the world and action.
