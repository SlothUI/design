About Me

Hey! I'm Sloth, a 21 year old Canadian designer and developer of 4 years, with 3 years dedicated to User Interface and User Experience design. I've been into games and creation all my life, now using Roblox to funnel my creativity and passion in order to share what I care about with others, while making it high quality and worthwhile for all players. I give a rather unique, more innovative artistic approach with functionality at the forefront on my designs, leading to an immersive and special identity for the games I work on.
